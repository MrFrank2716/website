Some of my Hobbies


"Everyone needs a hobby" | James Bond

10 Pin Bowling

Found a love for 10 Pin bowling just before the pandemic. I found it enjoyable and entertaining way to spend time with friends or by myself. Bowling is also a great way to relieve stress and have fun while being active. It is a sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels, and it provides a great opportunity to socialize and meet new people - this is one of the reasons why I love it, it's inclusive! With the variety of different balls and accessories available, its always an evolving sport.

MCParks

During the pandemic in late 2019, I had found a Minecraft server in my list that I never got around to visiting. Gladly I did and I have since joined an absolutely wonderful communtiy which has provided life long friends and skills. I have been volunteering since 08/10/2020 and it has been the best journey yet. I have the fabulous positions of Guest Relations, Imagineer, Parks Experience - Pixel Artist.

Blender 3D

I use Blender - a free powerful opensource application that is a very versatile tool for almost anybody. I use it for rendering and animation but there is so much that you can do with it. I believe anyone can be an artist as it is a skill that can be developed just by playing around. I absolutely love Blender and spend quite a lot of my time in it. It's a program that should be in everyone's pipeline.

Badminton

I have been playing badminton as a pasttime for many years but only recently joined a club where I play tournaments and competitions (I'm not the best) for fun. I make myself go and play these tournaments to challenge myself and put me out of my comfort zone.
